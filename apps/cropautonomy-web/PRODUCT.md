# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Farmers, growers, agronomists, field operations teams, agricultural businesses, and research institutions evaluating an autonomous agriculture platform. They land here from a referral, a search, or a partner mention, often on a phone in the field. They want to understand what CropAutonomy is, when it ships, and whether to give us their contact details. They will not read long-form copy and will not tolerate marketing that overstates hardware readiness.

## Product Purpose

`cropautonomy.com` is the public landing for the CropAutonomy platform: autonomous agricultural intelligence, field operations, AI crop analysis, and (future) device management. The site exists to (1) establish brand credibility with growers and ag businesses, (2) communicate the platform's roadmap honestly (prototype target August 2026, nothing shipped yet), and (3) capture qualified leads via the shared `capturePublicLead` flow.

Field Capture (phone capture and bulk upload) is the first input method being built, but it is one of several messaging pillars, not the centerpiece. Success is qualified lead volume from the target audience, not raw traffic.

## Brand Personality

Industrial, agricultural, precise, calm. The voice is confident, grounded, technically credible, clear, practical, and future-facing. Confidence rooted in real hardware and real workflows, not SaaS optimism. Roadmap framing for anything unshipped, future tense for Field Capture and the GAIA device lineup.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS templates (the hero-metric template, identical card grids, gradient accents as identity)
- Playful consumer visuals or soft startup styling
- Neon gaming aesthetics
- Decorative gradients as the main identity
- Fake screenshots that imply unavailable functionality
- Editorial / magazine-typographic aesthetics (this is operations, not content)
- Hype without substance, vague "AI-powered everything" claims
- Present-tense functional claims for Field Capture before it ships
- Framing CropAutonomy as a mobile-first phone scouting app

## Design Principles

1. **Honest about what ships.** No fake screenshots. Roadmap tense for unshipped capability. Prototype-target dates stated, not implied.
2. **Field-credible, not desk-credible.** Designed for real growers in real conditions. Outdoor legibility, mobile-first reading, plain language over jargon.
3. **Tools, not theater.** Motion, ornament, and scroll-driven effects earn their place by serving comprehension. Restrained by default.
4. **Multi-tenant by default.** Never imply single-farm or single-org assumptions, even in marketing copy or imagery.
5. **Precision over polish.** Calm density and crisp hierarchy beat marketing flourish. The reader should feel they are looking at infrastructure, not a pitch deck.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.2 AA. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`. Never indicate state with color alone. Touch targets sized for gloved or one-handed phone use. Body line length 65 to 75ch. Lead capture form must be fully keyboard-navigable with visible focus states and explicit error association.
