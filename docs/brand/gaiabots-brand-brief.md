# GaiaBots Brand Brief

## Brand Role

GaiaBots is the robotics and device brand for the CropAutonomy ecosystem.

It should communicate:

- upcoming autonomous agricultural hardware
- GAIA-R rover systems
- GAIA-D drone systems
- field robotics
- device knowledge and technical documentation
- hardware development progress

## Audience

- agricultural operators interested in robotics
- research partners
- hardware collaborators
- robotics engineers
- field teams
- early adopters

## Initial Positioning

GaiaBots is coming soon and under active development.

The first public site should focus on upcoming hardware, especially:

- GAIA-R ground rover platform
- GAIA-D autonomous aerial drone platform

The site should not imply that hardware is commercially available before it is ready.

## Voice

The voice should be:

- precise
- technical
- exploratory
- credible
- field-aware

Avoid:

- toy-like robot branding
- sci-fi exaggeration
- vague hardware promises
- consumer gadget styling

## Landing Page Messaging Pillars

- Upcoming agricultural robotics systems
- GAIA-R for ground-level crop intelligence
- GAIA-D for aerial field intelligence
- Built to connect with CropAutonomy
- Future home of device knowledge and documentation
- Prototype target: August 2026 where applicable
