# CropAutonomy Brand Brief

## Brand Role

CropAutonomy is the primary platform brand.

It should communicate:

- autonomous agricultural intelligence
- operational reliability
- practical field value
- AI-assisted crop insight
- future robotics readiness
- trust for farms and agricultural organizations

## Audience

- farmers
- growers
- agricultural businesses
- agricultural groups
- research institutions
- agronomists
- field operations teams

## Initial Positioning

CropAutonomy is coming soon and under active development.

It should be presented as a platform being built for:

- crop intelligence
- field monitoring
- AI scan workflows
- future autonomous systems
- multi-tenant agricultural operations

## Voice

The voice should be:

- confident
- grounded
- technically credible
- clear
- practical
- future-facing

Avoid:

- hype without substance
- overpromising autonomous capability
- generic startup language
- vague "AI-powered everything" claims

## Landing Page Messaging Pillars

In priority order — the first three are the dominant brand identity; Field Capture earns a mention as the named first capture method being built, not as a centerpiece.

- Autonomous agricultural intelligence
- Built for farms, fields, and field teams
- The GAIA device lineup (rovers, drones, sensors, edge compute, actuators) as the long-term direction
- AI crop analysis and environmental insight (planned)
- Robotics-ready platform foundation
- **Field Capture** — phone capture and bulk upload, the first capture method being built, the visible loop in the Aug 2026 prototype. Mention it; do not center the brand on it.
- Prototype target: August 2026
- Coming soon / active development (whole platform, including Field Capture — nothing is shipped yet)

Hard NOs for CropAutonomy copy:

- do not use present-tense functional claims for Field Capture ("scout with the camera in your pocket today", "get back disease pressure now"). Future tense / roadmap framing only.
- do not frame CropAutonomy as a mobile-first product or a phone scouting app
- do not contrast Field Capture (as "available") against GAIA-R/D (as "coming") — both are in development
- do not introduce "GAIA-U" / "GAIA-User" naming for phone capture
